About Me

My Journey

Hello! I'm Daniel Amoako-Frimpong, a passionate Computer Science graduate student at USC and a dedicated member of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE). My journey began in Ghana, where I discovered my love for technology through robotics and STEM education at Presbyterian Boys' Secondary School.

After earning my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology with First Class Honours from Ghana Communication Technology University, I made the transition to the United States to pursue my Master's in Computer Science at USC. This journey has been fueled by my passion for creating technology that makes a meaningful impact.

As a STEM educator and web developer, I'm committed to bridging the digital divide and inspiring the next generation of technologists. I'm actively working to increase diversity in technology and provide opportunities for underrepresented students to excel in STEM fields.
